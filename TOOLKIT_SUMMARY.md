### 10. **Formulas Reference** - Complete Formula Guide
Comprehensive reference for **15 key investment formulas:**

2. **After Repair Value (ARV)** - Estimated value after repairs
3. **70% Rule** - Quick MAO for flips
4. **Net Operating Income (NOI)** - Income after expenses
5. **Cap Rate** - Unlevered yield on property
6. **Cash-on-Cash Return** - Annual cash return on investment
7. **Gross Rent Multiplier (GRM)** - Price to rent ratio
8. **Rent-to-Value Ratio** - 1% rule checker
9. **Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR)** - Income vs debt coverage
10. **Break-Even Ratio** - Required occupancy to cover costs
11. **Return on Investment (ROI)** - Total gain/loss
12. **Monthly Cash Flow** - Net monthly income
13. **Total Return** - Cash + appreciation + principal
14. **Loan-to-Value (LTV)** - Loan as % of value
15. **Operating Expense Ratio** - Expenses as % of income

Each formula includes:
