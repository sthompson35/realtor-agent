Core Investment Strategies
Buy & Hold

The foundation of wealth building through real estate. Focus on cash flow and long-term appreciation.

Best For: Long-term investors, stable markets
Risk Level: Low to Moderate
Time Horizon: 5-10+ years
Fix & Flip

Purchase undervalued properties, renovate, and sell for profit. Requires construction expertise and market timing.

Best For: Experienced investors, hot markets
Risk Level: High
Time Horizon: 3-9 months
BRRRR Strategy

Buy, Rehab, Rent, Refinance, Repeat. Maximize cash flow through strategic refinancing.

Best For: Value-add opportunities
Risk Level: Moderate
Time Horizon: 6-12 months
Wholesaling

Find motivated sellers, get properties under contract, and assign contracts to other investors for fee.

Best For: New investors, low capital
Risk Level: Low
Time Horizon: 30-90 days
Market-Specific Approaches
Growth Markets
  • Focus on emerging suburbs with job growth
  • Target first-time homebuyer markets
  • Invest in workforce housing
  • Consider development opportunities
Stable Markets
  • Emphasis on cash flow and stability
  • Target institutional-quality properties
  • Focus on property management efficiency
  • Build long-term relationships
Value-Add Markets
  • Identify properties needing repositioning
  • Target management-intensive assets
  • Focus on operational improvements
  • Consider adaptive reuse opportunities
